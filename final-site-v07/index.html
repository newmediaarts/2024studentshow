Tuesday, May 14th | 4:30-6:30pm | @Kapiʻolani Community College

Location Details

[Film Title]

Kapiʻolani New Media Arts Animation cohort's capstone project taps into world culture in this year's Practicum short, [Film Title]. Through their combined efforts, five animators—Eric Chituck, Ian Hill, Emily Padia, Janae Taclas, and Amanda Young—collaborated for just over a year to breathe life into their work. Their efforts are a testament to the creativity and dedication of the animators, under the guidance and support of their dedicated instructor, Jake Shimabuku.

We kindly invite you to immerse yourself in the world of our 2024 New Media Arts Animation Practicum short film, [Film Title]. Should you wish to share your thoughts, questions or comments, please feel free to contact us at animate@hawaii.edu.

About KCC New Media Arts

KCC New Media Arts (NMA) is a two year AS degree program located at Kapi‘olani Community College in Honolulu, Hawai‘i. The NMA mission is to prepare students for employment in the fields of interface design and animation.
